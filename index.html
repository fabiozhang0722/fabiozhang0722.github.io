Brief Bio

Currently, I'm a staff scientist at PAII-Labs. Previously, I was a research scientist at NVIDIA, a visiting fellow at National Institutes of Health (NIH), and a postdoc at University of Iowa. I received my PhD at Zhejiang University, China in 2013.


My research is at the intersection of medical imaging, machine learning, and oncology, with the main interest in developing and validating automated medical image analysis algorithms for preventive and precision medicine.
