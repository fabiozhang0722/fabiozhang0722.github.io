Brief Bio

Currently, I'm a senior staff algorithm engineer at Alibaba DAMO Academy USA. Previously, I was a a staff scientist at PAII-Labs, a research scientist at NVIDIA, a visiting fellow at National Institutes of Health (NIH), and a postdoc at University of Iowa. I received my PhD at Zhejiang University, China in 2013.


My research focuses on developing and applying machine (deep) learning-based tools that have the potential to improve the screening, diagnosis, and treatment of diseases (mainly oncology and cadiovascular) - by analyzing medical images and clinical data.
